# Disclaimer

This is a **reference implementation / concept demonstrator**, not a regulated
company-formation service.

- The **government-registry, KYC, banking and licensing** steps are
  **simulations**. Identifiers they produce (CIN, EIN, GSTIN, TIN, SEC reg no.,
  permit numbers, …) are realistically formatted but are **not real
  registrations** and have no legal effect.
- The **payments, AI strategy, sanctions-screening and domain** steps make real
  calls to third-party services when configured, against their sandboxes/free
  tiers.
- Real incorporation involves legal documents, signatures, government fees and
  often licensed intermediaries (company secretaries, registered agents,
  attorneys). **Consult a qualified professional before filing** anything in any
  jurisdiction.
- Nothing here is legal, tax or financial advice.

Use it as the integration backbone for a real product — and replace each
simulated step with a vetted, compliant live integration before going to market.
